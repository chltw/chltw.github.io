Ching-Hua Lee

AI Center-Mountain View, Samsung Research, Mountain View, CA, USA

My name is Ching-Hua Lee and I work on a number of subjects in machine learning and signal processing.

  • I currently serve as a Senior Machine Learning Researcher at AI Center-Mountain View within Samsung Research.
  • I completed my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at UC San Diego in 2020 under the supervision of Prof. Bhaskar D. Rao, an Ericsson Endowed Chair and Distinguished Professor, and Dr. Harinath Garudadri.
  • I received my B.S. degree from the Department of Electrical Engineering at National Taiwan University in 2013.

My research focuses on efficient machine learning and signal processing solutions for speech and audio applications, including Single/Multi-Channel Speech Enhancement, Keyword Spotting, Speech Recognition/Translation and Speech-LLMs. During my Ph.D. in UC San Diego, I worked on adaptive filtering research with applications to speech processing in hearing aids.
